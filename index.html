WINE IMPORTS

We source exquisite wines from family-run vineyards across Europe, connecting their legacy to yours.

We believe the most memorable wines come from small, family-run vineyards where heritage is honored and every bottle tells a story.

Featured Producer: Bodega Peñafalcón

Bodega Peñafalcón is a family winery in Peñafiel, the heart of Spain's Ribera del Duero region. Founded by Casimiro Marcos and María José Arranz, the estate is known for producing bold, expressive Tempranillo wines that balance power with finesse. Their approach combines organic vineyard practices, meticulous hand selection, and long barrel aging to bring out the depth and character of the region's limestone soils.

We bring authentic estate-grown wines to New York

Based in the greater New York area, we serve a vibrant community of wine lovers spanning the city, Nassau County, and upstate. Our proximity to one of the world's most dynamic food and wine markets allows us to bring distinctive European wines directly to the restaurants, shops, and tables that value authenticity and craftsmanship. From Manhattan to the Hudson Valley, we connect local retailers and sommeliers with small, family-run producers whose wines reflect a sense of place and tradition.
